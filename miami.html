South Beach

beach · Outdoor

The signature Miami swim: a wide Atlantic beach backed by Ocean Drive, palms, cafés and Art Deco streets. Use it for the full visitor impression, but check wind, flags, heat and parking before committing.

beach · Outdoor

The easiest Miami Beach card for visitors: sand, promenade, volleyball/fitness energy and Ocean Drive one block away. It is not secluded, but it is simple, photogenic and practical.
