Where to swim in Zürich

Zürich has 17 swim spots worth knowing: 6 swimming pools, 5 lake baths, 3 wild swimming spots, 2 beaches and 1 thermal bath. Entry runs from CHF 8 to CHF 8, and 3 spots are free. Each one below lists opening times, prices, facilities and transport.

A signature left-bank Zürich badi: floating decks, lake lanes, after-work energy and a sunset angle over the water. It is the right pick when you want a proper urban swim with a social edge, not just a quick dip.
