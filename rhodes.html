Where to swim in Rhodes

Rhodes has 4 swim spots worth knowing: 3 beaches and 1 thermal bath. Entry runs from EUR 0 to EUR 5, and 3 spots are free. Each one below lists opening times, prices, facilities and transport.

Elli Beach

beach · Outdoor

A central Rhodes Town beach with fast access, clear water and a lively promenade. It works when you want a swim close to hotels, cafés and the old town rather than a remote cove.

Address
Rhodes Town waterfront
Entry
Free
Opening
Open beach; organised services vary by season.
Season
Main beach season May to October; busiest July and August.
Setting
Outdoor
Public transport
Rhodes town bus stops · Walkable from Mandraki

Anthony Quinn Bay

beach · Outdoor

A compact rocky bay with a postcard look and clear water. Come for scenery and snorkelling rather than lots of sand or space; the bay can feel busy because it is small.

Address
near Faliraki, Rhodes

Faliraki Beach

beach · Outdoor

A long resort beach with simple logistics: food, watersports, sunbeds and room to choose a section. Reliable for families and groups, less special for quiet-cove seekers.

Address
Faliraki, Rhodes
Entry
Free
Opening

Kallithea Springs

thermal bath · Outdoor

A polished coastal complex around restored spring architecture and small bathing coves. Choose it for atmosphere, photos and facilities rather than a wild-beach feeling.

Address
Kallithea, Rhodes
Entry

Opening
