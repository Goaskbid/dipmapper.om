What DipMapper does with your data

DipMapper.com helps you find places to swim. It uses your browser location only after you grant permission, or the destination text you type. DipMapper does not create accounts and does not store your precise location on a DipMapper server.

Local storage

Your privacy choice, recent searches and interface preferences are stored on your own device using local storage. This data never leaves your browser and is not used for tracking.

Third-party services

To show maps, weather, photos, routing and nearby places, your coordinates or destination text may be sent to OpenStreetMap and the Overpass API (map data), Open-Meteo (weather), Wikimedia Commons and Wikidata (photos and place information), and OSRM / FOSSGIS (driving times). These providers process the request in order to return a result.

Advertising (Google AdSense)

DipMapper.com uses Google AdSense to display advertising.

  • Third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this and other websites.
  • Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to you based on your visits to this site and/or other sites on the Internet.
  • You may opt out of personalised advertising via Google Ads Settings.
  • You may opt out of third-party vendor cookies at aboutads.info/choices.
  • More information: How Google uses information from sites that use its services.

Consent

Advertising and analytics storage are set to denied by default using Google Consent Mode v2, and are enabled only if you choose “Allow optional” in the privacy banner. Choosing “Essential only” keeps them disabled.
