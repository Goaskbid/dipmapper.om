# DipMapper

DipMapper helps people discover swimming spots using real-world conditions rather than guesswork.

The platform analyzes weather forecasts, water quality, crowd patterns, accessibility, parking availability, local facilities, and environmental conditions to help users choose where to swim before they arrive.

## Features

- Swimming spot discovery
- Weather intelligence
- Water quality analysis
- Crowd estimation
- Parking information
- Accessibility insights
- Family-friendly filtering
- Safety indicators
- Location intelligence
- AI recommendations

## Who Uses DipMapper

- Families
- Wild swimmers
- Tourists
- Open water swimmers
- Outdoor enthusiasts
- Weekend travelers
