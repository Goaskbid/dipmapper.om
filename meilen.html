Where to swim in Meilen

Meilen has 9 swim spots worth knowing: 8 lake baths and 1 swimming pool. Entry runs from CHF 0 to CHF 8, and 1 spot is free. Each one below lists opening times, prices, facilities and transport.

Hallenbad Allmend Meilen

swimming pool · Indoor

Meilen’s reliable all-weather swim option belongs at the top of a local search: a 25 m indoor pool, teaching water, sauna, bistro and accessible pool lift above the lake village. It solves the rainy-day or winter swim instead of pretending every good swim needs an outdoor badi.

Address
Toggwilerstrasse 38, 8706 Meilen
Entry
CHF 8 (child 4)
Opening
Mon-Fri 06:00-21:00; Sat-Sun 08:00-19:00. Holiday and event exceptions are published by Gemeinde Meilen.
Season
Year-round indoor pool, closed 11 July-16 August 2026 for revision/summer break according to the operator schedule.
Operator
Gemeinde Meilen

Strandbad Dorfmeilen

lake bath · Outdoor

Dorfmeilen is the proper village lake bath: lawn, rafts, warm food, playground and a direct right-bank Zürichsee feeling. It is the obvious local choice for a real summer badi day in Meilen rather than an anonymous shoreline stop.

Seestrasse 720, 8706 Meilen

Pre/post-season 2026: Mon 11:00-19:00, Tue-Sun 10:00-19:00. Main season 2026: Mon 11:00-20:00, Tue-Sun 09:00-20:00, weather permitting.

Badeanlage Ländeli

lake bath · Outdoor

Ländeli is a simple Obermeilen/Dollikon lakeside bathing area, not Steinrad and not a city badi. It is best for a short local dip, with free access and a clear operator warning that swimming and use are at your own risk.

Address
Seestrasse 950, 8706 Meilen
Entry
Free
Opening
Freely accessible; changing rooms and WC open during the municipal bathing season in good weather.
Season
1 May-14 September 2026 season signal from Gemeinde Meilen; no lifeguard at Ländeli.
Operator
Gemeinde Meilen
Setting
Outdoor

Badeanlage Steinrad

lake bath · Outdoor

Steinrad is Herrliberg’s lake bathing area north-west of Meilen. It must stay separate from Ländeli: different municipality, different coordinates, different access expectations and a much more local Herrliberg character.
