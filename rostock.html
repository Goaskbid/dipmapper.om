Strand Warnemünde

beach · Outdoor

The classic Rostock seaside choice: a broad Baltic beach beside the old fishing village, lighthouse, promenade and S-Bahn connection. Pick it when you want a proper holiday beach without leaving the city region.

Strand Hohe Düne

beach · Outdoor

A calmer beach alternative across the Warnow from Warnemünde. It suits visitors who want Baltic sand, marina context and a slightly less central feel, with ferry logistics as part of the trip.

Strand Markgrafenheide

beach · Outdoor

A wider, more nature-facing Rostock beach east of the river mouth. It is better for a full beach day and space than a quick city dip, but parking and summer traffic need planning.

Hallenschwimmbad Neptun

swimming pool · Indoor

The reliable Rostock indoor option for training, laps and bad-weather swimming. It is not a holiday beach; it is the practical city pool when Baltic wind or cold water make the beach unattractive.

Kopernikusstraße 17, 18057 Rostock
