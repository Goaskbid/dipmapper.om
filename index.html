How it works

DipMapper opens around your current search radius, keeps the map visible, and shows useful swim choices first. Tap a list card or numbered map dot for photos, weather, prices, facilities, parking and source links in one travel card.

Legal and data protection

Location is optional and processed in the browser for nearby search. External providers may receive coordinates or destination text when maps, weather, photos, routes or review links are used.
